Sunil Chhetri

Indian FA Captain.
Player Bengaluru FC.

Sunil Chhetri (born 3 August 1984) is an Indian professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains both Indian Super League club Bengaluru and the India national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest Indian players of all time, Chhetri is the fifth-highest international goalscorer among active players, behind only Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi, the joint fifth-highest of all time, and also both the most-capped player and the all-time top goalscorer for India.For more information click on More Details.
